About Us

Know about us More

Mapping consultants is a Delhi based Architecture and Interior design firm, established in 2002. We are supported by young as well as experienced, dedicated and committed team of Architects, Interior designer and consultants who are akin to a quick and yet a quality Work. Mapping consultants have wide expertise in the Educational, Industrial, Health, Corporate office and Residential Space designing. Our capability, therefor, is not just defined by credible conceptualization and designing with providing a positive environment to aid execution of work well in time. The testimony to our firm is the repeat business from our existing client and a positive word of mouth from them for our new business acquisition. Mapping Consultants designs are creative, functional, futuristic, invariable environmental and budget friendly with feel good factor. We also don’t believe in a one size fits. We undertake every type of Architecture & interior projects and each solution is custom designed to suits. Every design from the smallest balustrade detail to entire project are uniquely treated and designed to precisely serve their individual purpose with the latest technology and equipment. This environment, not only enables our workable and technically designed drawing but also has been aiding us successfully in completing any complex project thus far.

  • The Chief Architect of the firm is Registered with Council of Architects, New Delhi & Indian Institute of Architects. We have a numbers of other qualified & experienced staff in the field of Architecture & Interior Designing & PMC Projects.

The Services We Provide


ARCHITECTURE

Our architecture is driven by a belief that the quality of our surroundings has a direct influence on the quality of our lives, whether at home, in the workplace, the cultural building or the public realm. The same thinking applies at the larger scale of infrastructure, whether that is a commercial building, Residential building, Affordable Housing or anything else.

INTERIOR DESIGN

We believe that architecture should provide a seamless experience, from outside to inside. The starting point is to gain an understanding of a client’s needs and the way in which a room, or sequence of spaces will be used. We have created interiors that span the spectrum from private houses to office towers. The quality of light, the choice of materials, the use of colour, and the selection of furnishings and works of art are coordinated as part of the overall scheme; and each element involves the same process of questioning and refinement as the building itself.

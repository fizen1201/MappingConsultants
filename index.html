About Us


We offers Consultancy services in the field of Architectural, Interiors, Structural Detailing, external developments and its allied services (MEP). It excels in the field of Architecture, Interior and Furniture designs of Corporate Offices, Banks, Commercial, Religious, Industrial & Institutional Building & Large residential Township Projects. We also design projects for Green Building concepts (SAV GRIHA & BEE norms). The Chief Architect of the firm is Registered with Council of Architects, New Delhi & Indian Institute of Architects. We have a numbers of other qualified & experienced staff in the field of Architecture & Interior Designing & PMC Projects.
